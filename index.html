Listening

Albums I’ve spent time with. Click to expand.

A Tribe Called Quest

Listened: March 2024 • Rating: 9/10

Jazz-rap at its finest. The flows, the samples, the chemistry—everything clicks. A blueprint for how hip-hop could sound thoughtful and fun at the same time.

Radiohead

Listened: January 2024 • Rating: 9/10

The warmth and intimacy Radiohead rarely allows. Every track feels necessary. "Reckoner" and "Weird Fishes" are career peaks.

Frank Ocean

Listened: November 2023 • Rating: 10/10

Few albums capture longing and memory like this. The production is sparse but every sound has weight. A modern classic.

Nujabes

Listened: August 2024 • Rating: 8/10

Posthumous release that carries his legacy. Lo-fi, jazz, and soul woven into something peaceful. Perfect for late nights.

Daft Punk

Listened: May 2024 • Rating: 8/10

A love letter to disco and studio craftsmanship. Every groove is immaculate. "Touch" is one of the most ambitious tracks they ever made.

Miles Davis

Listened: February 2024 • Rating: 10/10

Modal jazz perfected. "So What" and "All Blues" are foundational. Still sounds fresh decades later.

Sufjan Stevens

Listened: June 2024 • Rating: 9/10

Orchestral folk with unexpected depth. "Chicago" and "John Wayne Gacy, Jr." show his range—joyful and haunting in equal measure.

Steely Dan

Listened: October 2024 • Rating: 8/10

Studio perfectionism at its peak. Jazz-rock with pristine production. "Deacon Blues" is a masterclass in arrangement.
